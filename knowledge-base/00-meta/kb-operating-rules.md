# KB Operating Rules

## Purpose

Keep this KB continuously useful for operating decisions, not just reference.

## Update cadence

- **Weekly (30 min):** refresh tactical playbooks based on delivery/sales lessons.
- **Monthly (60 min):** update KPI thresholds and benchmarks.
- **Quarterly (90 min):** rewrite strategy assumptions and retire stale guidance.

## Content standard (for new pages)

Every page should contain:

1. **When to use this**
2. **Operating model** (step-by-step)
3. **Checklist**
4. **KPIs & thresholds**
5. **Failure modes**
6. **References** (external links where relevant)

## Decision hygiene

For major decisions (hiring, offer launch, pricing change), document:

- Decision statement
- Assumptions
- Leading indicators
- Kill/continue criteria
- Review date

## Evidence policy

- Prefer original sources (research institutions, standards bodies, primary docs).
- Distinguish between **evidence** (data-backed) and **heuristics** (operator judgment).
- Attach links for non-obvious claims and benchmark thresholds.
