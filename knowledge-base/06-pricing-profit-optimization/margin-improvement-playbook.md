# Margin Improvement Playbook

## When to use this

Use when revenue is growing but profits are flat, volatile, or shrinking.

## 30-60-90 operating plan

### Days 1-30: Diagnose

- Segment P&L by offer/client/type
- Identify margin leakage sources: discounts, scope creep, rework, staffing mismatch, tool spend
- Establish baseline contribution margins

### Days 31-60: Intervene

- Raise price floors on new deals
- Introduce scope change orders and redline policy
- Re-staff work to right-skill levels
- Standardize repeatable deliverables

### Days 61-90: Institutionalize

- Launch margin dashboard
- Set monthly margin review cadence
- Link compensation/incentives to healthy margin + quality outcomes

## High-leverage moves

- Replace custom one-offs with modular offer blocks
- Improve discovery quality to cut downstream rework
- Tighten proposal assumptions and exclusions
- Negotiate annual vendor/tool contracts where usage is stable

## KPI dashboard

- Gross margin % (overall + by offer)
- Contribution margin per engagement
- Rework hours as % of total hours
- Effective bill rate by role
- Write-offs and unbilled hours

## Failure modes

- Cost-cutting that harms delivery quality and retention
- Margin targets set without delivery/system changes
- Incentives tied only to top-line revenue
