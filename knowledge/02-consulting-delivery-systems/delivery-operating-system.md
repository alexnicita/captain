# Consulting Delivery Operating System

## When to use this

Use to run delivery at high quality while maintaining margins and predictable throughput.

## Core operating cadence

- **Daily (15 min):** team standup on blockers and client commitments
- **Weekly (60 min):** engagement health review (scope, risks, impact)
- **Biweekly:** client outcome review tied to agreed metrics
- **Monthly:** utilization + margin + quality retrospective

## Delivery stack

1. **Intake standardization**
   - Structured kickoff: objectives, constraints, stakeholders, success metrics.
2. **Workstream decomposition**
   - Separate discovery, decision, build, enablement tracks.
3. **Decision records**
   - Log major tradeoffs with owner/date/revisit trigger.
4. **Quality gates**
   - Before client delivery: peer review, evidence check, implementation feasibility check.
5. **Knowledge capture**
   - Convert each engagement into reusable methods/templates.

## Engagement health scorecard (weekly)

Rate 1-5:

- Sponsor engagement
- Scope stability
- Delivery confidence
- Data/instrumentation quality
- Outcome trajectory

Escalate any dimension <=2.

## KPIs

- On-time milestone completion: **>85%**
- Gross margin by engagement: **>50-60% target band**
- Utilization (billable for consulting staff): **70-85% sustainable band**
- Rework ratio: **<15%** of delivery hours
- Client health score (CSAT/NPS proxy): trend improving quarter over quarter

## Failure modes

- Unclear success metrics at kickoff
- Hidden scope creep and unpriced change requests
- Hero-based delivery with no process reliability
- No structured closure and knowledge extraction
