# Balancing Client Services and Venture Building

## When to use this

Use when a studio funds ventures with consulting revenue and needs disciplined allocation.

## Operating model

1. **Set explicit split targets**
   - Example bands: 60-80% client revenue support, 20-40% venture investment.
2. **Ring-fence venture capacity**
   - Protect a fixed allocation of top talent for venture progress.
3. **Define transfer pricing**
   - Shared staff time charged to ventures at transparent internal rates.
4. **Quarterly rebalance**
   - Increase venture share only when client engine health is stable.

## Decision rules

- If client margin < target for 2+ months, pause venture scaling.
- If venture hits gate milestone ahead of plan, consider incremental allocation.
- Never fund ventures through hidden overwork on client teams.

## KPIs

- Client engine gross margin and cash conversion
- Venture milestone attainment rate
- Talent allocation drift vs plan
- Founder/lead bandwidth saturation indicators

## Failure modes

- Ventures starved indefinitely by short-term client demands
- Client quality degradation due to over-allocation to ventures
- Blurred accounting and false profitability narratives
