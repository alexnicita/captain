# Elite Product Consultancy: Growth Engine

## When to use this

Use to build predictable pipeline without relying on referrals alone.

## Operating model (weekly)

1. **Point of view publication** (1 artifact/week)
   - Publish one sharp insight from active client work (sanitized).
2. **Founder-led distribution**
   - Share to owned channels; run direct outreach to ideal buyers.
3. **Conversion asset**
   - Route interest to a specific diagnostic offer with clear scope/outcomes.
4. **Pipeline review**
   - Track stage conversion and reasons for loss.
5. **Proof compounding**
   - Convert wins into case narratives and tactical frameworks.

## Simple funnel design

- Insight content → short consult call → paid diagnostic → sprint proposal → expansion

## Checklist

- ICP is specific enough to name in one sentence
- Distinct method/framework documented and reusable
- Sales call script includes disqualification criteria
- Proposal template ties scope to measurable outcomes
- CRM stages include loss taxonomy

## KPIs

- Inbound qualified leads/month
- Paid diagnostic conversion rate
- Diagnostic-to-sprint conversion rate
- Average sales cycle duration
- Expansion revenue within 6 months

## Failure modes

- Posting content with no buyer-specific thesis
- Discovery calls with no qualification rigor
- Proposals listing tasks, not outcomes
- No post-mortem on lost deals
