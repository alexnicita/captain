# Weekly Business Review (WBR) Template

## Purpose

Single operating meeting to align strategy, sales, delivery, and finance signals.

## Duration

60-75 minutes.

## Agenda

1. **Scoreboard (10 min)**
   - Top-line: pipeline, revenue, margin, delivery health, cash
2. **Bottleneck diagnosis (15 min)**
   - What is currently constraining growth/profit most?
3. **Domain reviews (25 min)**
   - Sales/GTM
   - Delivery/quality
   - Product/venture progress
4. **Decisions required (15 min)**
   - List decision owner + deadline
5. **Commitments (5 min)**
   - Top 3 actions before next WBR

## WBR checklist

- Metrics are updated before meeting
- Discussion references data, not anecdotes alone
- Decisions and owners logged live
- Unresolved issues moved to dedicated deep-dive sessions

## Core metric set

- Qualified pipeline value and coverage ratio
- Win rate and cycle time
- Delivery on-time % and client health
- Gross margin and contribution margin trend
- Cash runway and receivables status
