# Founder Operating System

## When to use this

Use to improve founder focus, decision quality, and execution consistency.

## Weekly founder cadence

- **Monday (60 min):** set top 3 outcomes for the week
- **Midweek (30 min):** bottleneck review and re-prioritization
- **Friday (45 min):** outcomes scored, lessons captured, next risks identified

## Decision framework (DACI-lite)

For non-trivial decisions:

- **Driver:** accountable owner
- **Approver:** final decision maker
- **Contributors:** required expert input
- **Informed:** downstream stakeholders

Add: decision deadline + reversal cost.

## Personal leverage system

1. Keep a "not now" list to prevent scope creep.
2. Delegate recurring low-leverage work with SOPs.
3. Reserve uninterrupted strategy blocks (2-3 per week).
4. Track energy management: sleep, focus hours, context switching.

## Checklist

- Quarterly priorities are explicit and limited
- Meeting load audited monthly
- Hiring decisions tied to bottlenecks, not anxiety
- Personal calendar reflects strategic intent
- Founder dashboard reviewed weekly

## KPIs

- % weekly priorities completed
- Time spent in deep work vs reactive work
- Decision cycle time for key issues
- Team clarity score (qualitative pulse)
- Burn multiple/runway trend (where applicable)

## Failure modes

- Reactive calendar with no strategic blocks
- Delayed decisions due to role ambiguity
- Founder becoming the bottleneck in approvals
- Underinvesting in team operating rhythm
